Parirenyatwa General Hospital

  • Contact Details:
  • Telephone:+263 24 2701555
  • Email:publicrelations@parihosp.org | parirenyatwapr@gmail.com
  • website:parihosp.org

Details:
The largest medical center in the entire Zimbabwe, the hospital was previously known as the Andrew Fleming Hospital.Along with its general medical and surgical sections, the hospital also has a maternity section. It specializes in eye treatment as well and has an annex for psychiatric patients. It also has several special pediatric wardsThe hospital has over 5000 beds, and 12 operating theatres in the main complex.The University of Zimbabwe’s College of Health Sciences is also located at Parirenyatwa. The university’s medical students continue their education here from the third year.

  • Telephone:+263 29 2252111
  • Email:drnbtdzvanga@ubh.org.zw | kelvinkaseke@ymail.com

Located in the Eastern part of the city of Bulawayo it is one of the most principal referral centers for the Southern part of Zimbabwe. The hospital serves an urban population of around one million At the time, the hospital had 300 beds, and at present, the number of beds is 650. There are also plans to extend the hospital, which will be done in different phases



Karanda Mission Hospital

  • Contact Details:
  • Telephone:+263774-082-211
  • website:karanda.org

Details:
Along with the hospital, the complex also has a nurse training school, with around 55 students. It also has a primary school meant for children from grade 1 to grade 7. A home-based care program is also provided by the hospital. It works with the Evangelical church, which is similar in doctrine to the Evangelical Free Church in the US. With a license for over 150 beds, an average day at the hospital sees between 10-20 surgeries along with around 250 outdoor patients



Mpilo Central Hospital

  • Contact Details:
  • Telephone:+263 29 2212011

Details:
In addition to the hospital, it also has a school of Nursing, and also a school of Midwifery. Both are located within the hospital campus itself. With a total of 1000 beds, this hospital which serves the population of Bulawayo and its nearby provinces also has links with the University of Zimbabwe, as well as other academic institutions. The hospital also employs 2300 staff across various services and is known for providing care and safety towards its patients
